History

E-ramen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It has been established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia in 2015. All Eramen menus are made with organic flour and turned into delicious ramen noodles. Customers can find any kind of ramen, such as shoyu or miso ramen and other side dishes such as gyoza and tempura.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure customers have the best eating experience.

Mission

Our mission is to Introduce the incredible tastes of Japanese cuisine to as many places in the world as possible.
